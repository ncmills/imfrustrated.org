# I'm Frustrated Dot Org — NY Nonprofit Incorporation Checklist

## Step 1: File Certificate of Incorporation
- [ ] Fill in blanks in `certificate-of-incorporation.md` (county, name, address, directors)
- [ ] **Get Supreme Court Justice approval** (N-PCL §404(a)) — required for charitable nonprofits
  - Petition a Justice of the Supreme Court in your judicial district for approval of the Certificate
  - The court will notify the NY Attorney General's Charities Bureau, who reviews and can object or request changes
  - The Justice signs an order approving the Certificate (typically 2-4 weeks)
- [ ] File the approved Certificate + court order with NY Department of State
  - Online: https://dos.ny.gov/forming-corporation-new-york-state
  - Filing fee: **$75**
  - Mail to: Division of Corporations, One Commerce Plaza, 99 Washington Avenue, Albany, NY 12231
  - **DOS will reject the filing without the court approval order attached**

## Step 2: Get an EIN (Free, Instant)
- [ ] Apply online at https://www.irs.gov/businesses/small-businesses-self-employed/apply-for-an-employer-identification-number-ein-online
- [ ] Select "View Additional Types" → "Nonprofit/Tax-Exempt Organization"
- [ ] You'll get the EIN immediately

## Step 3: Organizational Board Meeting
- [ ] Hold initial board meeting (can be informal, even over Zoom)
- [ ] Adopt the Bylaws
- [ ] Elect officers (President, Secretary, Treasurer)
- [ ] Authorize opening a bank account
- [ ] Take minutes — keep them in your records

## Step 4: File for Federal Tax-Exempt Status (501(c)(3))
- [ ] **Form 1023-EZ** (simplified) — if gross receipts expected under $50K/year and assets under $250K
  - File online at https://www.pay.gov (search "1023-EZ")
  - Fee: **$275**
  - Approval: typically 2-4 weeks
- [ ] OR **Form 1023** (full application) — if above thresholds
  - Fee: **$600**
  - Approval: 3-6 months

## Step 5: NY State Tax Exemption
- [ ] File **Form CT-247** with NY Department of Taxation and Finance for state tax exemption
  - https://www.tax.ny.gov/forms/corp_tax_forms.htm
  - Free to file
- [ ] File **Form ST-119.2** for sales tax exemption (optional, if buying goods)

## Step 6: NY Charities Registration
- [ ] Register with the NY Attorney General's Charities Bureau (CHAR500 annual filing)
  - Required if you solicit donations in NY
  - https://www.charitiesnys.com/
  - Initial registration: **Form CHAR410**

## Step 7: Other
- [ ] Open a bank account (need EIN + Certificate of Incorporation + Bylaws)
- [ ] File biennial statement with NY DOS every 2 years ($9 fee)
